About these datasets & columns

Shot Events datasets are derived from NHL play-by-play event data and prepared for location-based analysis, player shot-profile research, dashboards, and predictive modeling workflows.

Player Location Priors summarizes each player's historical shot-location distribution into rates such as inner slot, mid slot, circles, and perimeter share.

Shot Location Events contains granular shot-level records including event, team, player, goalie, coordinate, standardized coordinate, and shot-region fields.

Source data may be revised or updated over time.
